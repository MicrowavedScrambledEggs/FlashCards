Heart Anatomy: Connected Veins and Arteries

Aorta: Systemic artery. Blood pumped into it from left ventricle.
Pulmonary artery, from right ventricle to lungs.

Blood comes back to heart to the right atrium via the vena cava.
Superior vena cava is where blood from the head flows into the 
heart, inferior vena cava has blood from the body below the 
heart flow through.

Four pulmonary veins from lung to left atrium
