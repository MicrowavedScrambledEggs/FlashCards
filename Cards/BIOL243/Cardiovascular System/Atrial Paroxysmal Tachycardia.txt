Cardiovascular System
Atrial Paroxysmal Tachycardia

Abnormal

Paroxysmal means a series of rapid heart beats that suddenly 
start and then suddenly stop.

Can be stopped with a vagal reflex or quinidine or procainimide.
(low dose beta block)

P wave is inverted (goes down) if origin is near A-V node.

Occurs by re-entrant pathways. Part of the atria are depolarised
so get repolarised twice.

Can cause dizzyness etc
