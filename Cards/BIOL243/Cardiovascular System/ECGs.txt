BIOL243
Cardiovascular System
ECGs

Measure with 3 electrodes, one in left arm, one in right arm
and one in a leg. 

P wave: Atrial depolarization
QRS wave: ventricular depolarization (Atrial repolarization
            happens somewhere here)
T wave: Ventricular repolarization

P-R interval: 0.16 sec
Q-T interval: 0.35 sec
