Bradycardia

A slow heart rate usually less than 60 bpm.

Happens when relaxing/ sleeping.

Present in athletes who have a large stroke volume. More 

Can be caused by vagal stimulation (stimulation of the vagus 
nerve, the main parasypathetic nerve to the heart and the 
gastro-intestinal tract), one example of which is the carotid 
sinus syndrome.
