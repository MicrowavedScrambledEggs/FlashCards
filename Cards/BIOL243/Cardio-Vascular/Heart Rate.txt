BIOL243
Cardiovascular System
Heart Rate

Fast at birth, slows down getting to adult rates after puberty.

Typically the bigger the animal/heart, the slower the heart rate
as big hearts are harder to beat.

Neurogenic origin of heart rate (controled by nerve) in 
invertebrates.

Myogenic origin of heart rate (controled by muscle) in 
vertebrates, tunicates and molluscs.
