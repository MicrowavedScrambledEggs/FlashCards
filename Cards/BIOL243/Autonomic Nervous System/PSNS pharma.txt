Parasympathomimetic drugs:
    nicotine
        activates nicotinic receptors
    pilocarpine and methacholine
        activates muscarinic receptors, 
        causes e.g. profuse sweating

  Neostigmine, physostigmine (eserine)
     potentiates the effect of acetylcholine

Antimuscarinic drugs
      Atropine (belladonna) and scopolamine
      blocks the effect of acetylcholine on 
       effector cells
      causes pupillary dilation “belladonna”
